    We are a program...

    Learning El Salvador is a program created in 2018, and its main objective is to help young people in the subjects of English and values, since they are very important and useful subjects for the life of any person and they also offer many opportunities.

    This program was an award winner "El premio solidaridad" in the year 2018.
