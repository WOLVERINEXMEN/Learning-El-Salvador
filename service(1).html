    El Salvador

    Who are we?

    Learning El Salvador is a Social Project that began operations in April 2018 oriented in the teaching of English, practice of values and vocational guidance to young high school graduates of the National Institute of Colonia Ciudad Obrera de Apopa. Currently and since its inception, the base of volunteers in Learning are graduates of different promotions of Supérate Raíces. Learning El Salvador is one of the winners organizations of the Solidarity Award in the Center ¡Supérate! Fundación Raíces, which is given to organizations that create a project and are based on teaching and helping others.

    What do we want?

    Mission

    To provide young high school graduates in the municipality of Apopa with fundamental tools to develop their academic and professional skills for their continuous education and to obtain their first job.

    Vision

    To be a non-profit organization focused on academic and professional assistance to high school graduates of the municipality of Apopa so that they can aspire to opportunities that improve their quality of life and that of their families.

    Our Values

    Leadership

    This value is present in our Staff, we practice positive leadership with the students, and we seek their development in this value.

    Communication

    We have excellent student-teacher communication.

    Integrity

    We are well-rounded people, and we seek to form well-rounded young people.

    Assertiveness

    We are assertive in communicating with our students.

    Dynamism

    Our classes are dynamic, because the best way to learn is by playing.

    Commitment

    We are committed to do our best to provide dignified and constructive teaching.
